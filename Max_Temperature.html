Max Temperature

As expected, the closer you get to the equator (0 degrees latitude) the warmer the temperature. What is interesting to note is that the southern hemisphere (negative degrees latitude) tends to be warmer than the northern hemisphere this time of year. This is likely due to the tilt of the earth at the time the weather data was gathered and analyzed.
